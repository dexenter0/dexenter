DeXenter Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to a Safe Online Environment

Our Unwavering Commitment to Child Safety

At DeXenter, the safety and well-being of children are paramount. We are deeply committed to creating and maintaining an online environment that is safe, respectful, and free from all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have implemented robust measures and policies to prevent, detect, and respond to content and behavior that violates these standards.

We adhere strictly to all applicable laws and regulations regarding child safety and online protection. Our platform is designed with safeguards to protect our younger users and to ensure that any harmful content or activity is swiftly addressed.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

DeXenter has a zero-tolerance policy for content and behavior related to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Any depiction of child sexual abuse material (CSAM), including images, videos, or audio.
  • Content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers children.
  • Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes.
  • Grooming behavior targeting minors.
  • Content that promotes, glorifies, or facilitates CSAE.
  • Sharing or distribution of private or identifying information of minors without consent, especially in a harmful context.
  • Any content or behavior that violates laws related to child protection.

In-App Reporting Mechanisms

We empower our users to be an active part of maintaining a safe community. DeXenter provides clear and accessible in-app reporting tools that allow users to report any content, behavior, or accounts that they believe violate our child safety standards or terms of service.

To report a concern within the DeXenter app, please follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the content, profile, or message you wish to report.
  2. Look for the "Report" icon (often represented by three dots, a flag, or a shield icon).
  3. Select the most appropriate reason for your report (e.g., "Child Abuse," "Inappropriate Content," "Harassment").
  4. Provide any additional details that might help our moderation team investigate the report thoroughly.
  5. Submit the report.

All reports are treated with the utmost seriousness and confidentiality.

Moderation and Enforcement

DeXenter employs a dedicated team and utilizes advanced technological tools to actively monitor our platform for violations of our child safety policies. Our moderation process includes:

  • **Proactive Detection:** Using automated systems to identify and flag potentially harmful content.
  • **Reactive Review:** Promptly reviewing all user-submitted reports.
  • **Expert Human Review:** Trained moderators review flagged content and reports to make informed decisions.
  • **Swift Action:** Content found to violate our policies is removed immediately. Accounts involved in severe violations, especially those related to CSAE, are permanently banned.
  • **Response Time:** We are committed to reviewing and acting on child safety reports as quickly as possible.

Cooperation with Authorities

DeXenter is fully committed to cooperating with law enforcement and relevant national and international authorities in the investigation and prosecution of child sexual abuse and exploitation. We report suspected illegal activity to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) in the United States, and other relevant bodies globally, as required by law.

We respond to valid legal requests for information in accordance with applicable laws and our privacy policy.

Age Restrictions and Verification

DeXenter is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We implement self-declaration from user to prevent underage individuals from accessing our platform. If we become aware that a user is underage, we will take immediate action to terminate their account.
